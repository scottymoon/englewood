Kids

Englewood Kids has something for all ages. Our nursery accepts children birth through 4 years. Our preschool accepts children 4-5 years. Englewood KidZone is our ministry for kindergarten through fifth grade. It is a high energy service with vibrant worship, fun games, cool object lessons, skits & zany characters. Rest assured you can go to service and know your kids are in a safe place while learning about God and experiencing Him in a powerful way.

For more information send us an email:
kids@englewoodchurch.org
